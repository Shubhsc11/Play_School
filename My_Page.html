Classes

In Play School, There are 3-4 classes comes in it such as-
Preparatory Class(Pre-Nursery & Nursery) | KG-I(LKG) | KG-II(UKG)

In this class two classes consists:

Pre-nursery & Nursery

Child learn: Speaking, Playing, Seating in the classroom, take care of child etc.

Age min: 3 Years

This class is also known as LKG.

Child learn: Drawing, names of numbers, observing, listening, exploring, and asking questions etc.


Age min: 4 Years

This class is also known as UKG.

Child learn: Writing Properly, word knowledge, language development, verbal expression and social interaction and awareness etc.

Age min: 5 Years
